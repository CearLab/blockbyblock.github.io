About Block by Block workshop

Construction robotics is revolutionizing the building industry, with autonomous robots handling everything from site preparation—pushing and organizing materials like soil and gravel—to the precise assembly of modular structures.


This workshop will dive into the key challenges and innovations in the field, focusing on how multi-agent systems (MAS) can coordinate these tasks. Modular construction offers a scalable solution ideal for robots, but coordinating a team of heterogeneous robots requires sophisticated planning and scheduling.


We'll also explore push manipulation, where robots equipped with simple tools shape and organize materials for site prep, creating a solid foundation for modular assembly.


Join us to learn how to advance MAS coordination and construction robotics in real-world environments!
